Follow the white hiker
I've been interested in the idea of date me docs for a while, and thought I would make one of my own. But I wanted to make one less like a CV, and instead try to create a sense of what spending time together could be like. And also I figured this would be more fun for readers than reading another lengthy document.

So I've built a "choose your own adventure" story. Hope you enjoy!

But we all have our dealbreakers, so to save you the time from going on the rollercoaster of emotions that is my adventure story, some key facts that may disqualify me from some of you:

  • Location: I'm currently in the Canary Islands in Spain, off the coast of Morocco. It's a temporary set-up; I haven't yet decided how long I'll stay. Up until recently I lived in London, and might return there, but I'm ultimately pretty open-minded about where I'll end up.
  • Views on kids: I lean against having kids. Primarily because I already have so many things I want to do with my life, and think I will have more time to have a positive impact on the world if I don’t have kids. But like everything else on here, it’s not set in stone. I think I’d make a good dad!  There’s plenty I find exciting and inspiring about it. I used to work a lot with kids and really enjoyed it (not the same, I know!).
