Obligatory Headshot
Hi there! I'm on the market, and if you're looking at this, then I guess you are too. This should help us navigate those awkward first steps.

I'm looking for someone to live and love with long-term while we both support each other's goals. I've never been married and while I'd be happy with a lifetime partner, I don't know that marriage itself is the right setup for me. That said, I have been in a 5+ year relationship. In the meantime, let's just be nice to each other and test our compatibility. We can see if there's an intersect in what we're looking for and do what seems right in the meantime.

My ideal partner has obsessions and can't help but share them. She has clear goals and a plan to achieve them, even if they're hard as hell. (I'm talking to you, ambitious women other guys are afraid of.) She takes care of herself now, knowing our bodies get harder to maintain as we age. Also, no kids, religion, or perpetuating animal suffering through food choices. She wants to make a positive mark on the world.

I take on challenging goals. I'm mostly through the manuscript of a nonfiction book on a technical topic. I've published in both academic journals and popular media. I've also grown two nonprofits from scratch. I set up the org and funding mechanism that helped a long-active male contraceptive to a Phase I human clinical trial. I also facilitated campaigns to get a better voting method implemented in two cities, which had never been used in government elections before. I've raised millions of dollars for causes I care about. I enjoy being active and have a ton of different hobbies.

Location-wise, I can live anywhere for my work and recently chose Philadelphia after referencing a simple heat table I put together factoring in cost of living, walkability, transit, and bikability. I actively do not like cars and choose not to own one. I do have a license and can drive though. I have to do this visiting Kentucky during the holidays.

Other randomness. I care about financial independence. I budget for charitable giving and give publicly. I also love science and am rather methodical at most things I try my hand at. If youre as obsessed with learning, well probably get along great. Also, I try my best for clarity on all this, but if you're still nicking a particular box and things look otherwise good, I'd still be up for us talking and checking things out.
